Apple owns capture · Kerf wraps

Use-label discipline

  • Estimate-safe (green) · room dimensions for estimating, schematic, shop drawings · OK to use immediately
  • Verify before release (amber) · cabinetry, stone, glass, millwork · tape-verify required before fab/shop release · blocks downstream
  • Manual required (red) · scan unreliable here · take a hand measurement
  • Per the LiDAR precision canon · the use-label is on the measurement itself · downstream consumers (F-G1 Draft, fab orders) inherit the discipline

Cross-scope takeoffs (D-043 win)

One scan → multiple downstream estimates · per kerf_lidar_day_one_substrate.md:

  • HVAC · room volume feeds Manual J load calculation
  • Tile / flooring · square footage + waste factor
  • Cabinetry · linear feet (with verify required)
  • Electrical · existing fixtures detected
  • Plumbing · rough-in locations

The contractor doesn't have to take 5 separate measurements for 5 estimates. One scan, 5 takeoffs, all use-label tagged.
