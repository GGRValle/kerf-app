APR 23 ADDENDUM — ARCHITECTURE LOCKS (not yet wireframed in detail)

Blackboard = persistent memory + visible graphical surfaceArchitecture Principle #6. Every capture, decision, approval flows through the shared Blackboard. Permission-aware reads. V1 = thin wrapper over existing mobile/Slack surfaces. Full team-wide graphical Blackboard views land V2.0+.
Team platform — 4 audiences, 1 BlackboardOwner (mobile-dominant, this wireframe) · Office (desktop tandem, V1.5 Standard UI) · Field crew (capture-first, partial V1) · Subs (scoped portal, V2.0+). Manager of Operations named as scaling role — MoO surface V1.5-V2.0.
Role-aware CoS = V2.0+ directionV1 has one CoS (owner). V2.0 alpha adds MoO CoS. V2.1 paid beta adds PM + field super CoS. V2.2 GA settles sub portal. NOT wireframed in this doc — mentioned here so team doesn't scope-creep V1.
Adaptive CoS = product principle, not marketing claimCoS learns the owner over time — floats up the right info at the right moment. Never framed as "owner trains the AI." Demonstrate-before-claim.
Intake workflow — Project + Change Order (V1.5-V2.0)One architecture, two entry points. Multi-modal: form / voice / recorded-conversation-with-consent / text. Photos + editable LiDAR (V2.0 Alpha, pulled forward from V2.2 GA). CO Intake reuses engine with project-context pre-populated and camera+LiDAR primed at top of form for field quick-capture. Home-screen intake button (Project) + in-project CO button (both owner + field roles).
FinTech partner-first (V2.0-V2.1)5 primitives: homeowner pay (V2.0 Stripe/Square), purchasing (V2.0 Home Depot Pro API), crew cards (V2.1 Ramp white-label), job cards (V2.1 partner / V3.0+ in-house decision), homeowner financing (V2.1 GoodLeap referral, partner-forever). No fintech UI in V1.
V1 scope unchangedThis legend documents architectural locks only. V1 wireframes below are the same as Apr 21 — no new screens, no new surfaces. Kill-switch criteria unchanged.
"Decisions, not dashboards."Candidate tertiary tagline. Every surface must answer "what does the owner do next from this view?" not just display status. Pairs with "Make More." primary and the two anti-positioning lines.
