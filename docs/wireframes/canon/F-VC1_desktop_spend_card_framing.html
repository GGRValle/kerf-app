Why partner

F-VC1 · Spec

4-question rule

  • Macro / comparison / detail? Detail / framing preview.
  • List / grid / both? List only.
  • Operator cognitive job? Understand Kerf’s design posture for partner-integrated spend cards and transaction categorization.
  • What stays hidden until drill-down? Actual payment rails, partner banking UX, and deeper transaction ledger mechanics.

Why this exists

  • It marks the V1.5+ design position without pretending Kerf is building banking rails now.
  • It centers tagging, categorization confidence, and approval flow.
  • It keeps partner integration strategy explicit in the canon set.
What this enforcesKerf is partner-first on fintech rails. The product owns project context, categorization, and approvals — not the banking substrate.
What this refuses to beNot a hidden promise of in-house banking in V1, and not a sketch of a full accounting suite. This is a scoped framing surface.
