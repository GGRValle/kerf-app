Two rules the build must hold

1 · Travels, never parks. The bubble rides onto the artifact (one surface). No navigate-away-and-park — that caused the live jumpy oscillation (estimate↔convo↔estimate). One surface = no oscillation.
2 · Mic side = handedness setting. Docks right by default; a lefty flips to left in Settings (`micSide` prop from day one). Center idle is ambidextrous regardless.

This is the build target — supersedes the navigate-and-park behavior in #308/#310/#311 for the presentation layer. The intent seam (#311), estimator (runEstimate), store (Postgres), and policy gate are unchanged. Voice-edits stay source-labeled + nothing-silent; "file it" / "send" remain the hard consequence gates. Dark + light both.
