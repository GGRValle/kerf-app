Sidebar filter notes

  • Sales hidden: Sales aggregates aren't PM scope at SMB tier. Per-project sales-stage info accessible via Project Detail when relevant.
  • Money tagged "budget": Money destination opens to per-project budget vs. actual view. Margin · AR/AP detail · capital plans not surfaced.
  • Client Success hidden: Portfolio-level satisfaction aggregates aren't PM concern. Per-project CS signals visible via Project Detail when active.
  • Team & Ops visible: PM needs sub coordination + paperwork + procurement. Operator-private content (legal / HR / capital / attorney) not surfaced.
  • No top-nav Audit: Audit substrate is per-project (Audit sub-tab on Project Detail) only for PM. No global Audit destination — that's Owner / Settings-bound.
  • No tier-C hook: "Other business units" is Owner concern, not PM.
