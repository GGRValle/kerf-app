It's co-branded to the shop, leads with what needs the client's approval (selections, change orders, proposals), and shows their schedule, photos, documents, and messages. F-CP1 · Spec

Client-facing projection (D-060 §12)

  • Sibling of the Sub portal (F-SH2). The client logs into their own project only.
  • Co-branded to the shop ("GGR Design + Remodeling · powered by Right Hand").
  • Leads with what needs the client's approval.

Approvals live here

  • Selection approvals, change orders, proposals — the client-side consequence point.
  • Approve → propagates back: a Project Selection (§3) that drives the PO + Schedule; a signed CO.
  • Two-sided with the internal Clients (CRM) domain.

Isolation (tightest tier)

  • Client sees only their project — never other clients, your other jobs, costs, or margin.
  • Amounts shown are client-facing (allowance / contract price), never internal cost. Markup is never visible (existing canon).

Routing

  • Add Client as an external projection on the F-LND1 routing map.
