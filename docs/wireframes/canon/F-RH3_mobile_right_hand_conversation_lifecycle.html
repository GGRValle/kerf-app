The heart, tied into the frame

  • The conversation is one continuous gold-framed surface; its base IS the bottom bar (Home/Create · mic · Camera/More), all in the same frame — the mic is seated in the surface, never floating below it.
  • Tap the center mic → the surface blooms upward from it. The mic stays the dead-center anchor (universal thumb zone).
  • The other bar items stay faintly flanking the heart — it never leaves its home in the bar.

Flow (1→5)

  • Listen → talk → reply in place. Each utterance is a turn (You gray/right · RH gold/left), never a blob; thread auto-scrolls to the latest turn.
  • Composer: `+` left · text middle (Send appears on type) · mic the center heart. Voice + type, one lane.
  • The bloom grows from a sprout toward full-screen as the conversation accumulates.

Strict on the way out

  • Green consequence bubble only at the durable write (stage 4) — the real question, confirmable by tap or voice.
  • Filed · via voice only after the write returns — no fake "saved."
  • Conversation memory ≠ fake filing: RH can hold a spoken label but says so honestly until matched to a real job.
  • Permissive in · strict out (no durable write/send/money without the gate; no invented scope).
