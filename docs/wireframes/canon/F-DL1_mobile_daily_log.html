F-DL1 · Daily Log · who · where · a spoken note · media

Simple by design, and the corrected version of the v47 /field-capture surface (no heavy routing table, no confusing second mic). Four reads, top to bottom: who (auto), where (geo-located, or prefilled from the job they're clocked into — a manager arriving picks the job), the note (tap → Right Hand / Field Hand opens and you just talk), then photos · video · other. File it and it lands in that job's Daily Log. F-DL2 is the same surface wired to clock-out.

F-DL1 · Spec

The four reads

  • Who — auto from auth. No input.
  • Where — geo-locate + "clocked into this job" prefill for a field worker. A manager arriving off-GPS gets a "Which job?" picker here instead (the one field they fill). "Change" always available.
  • Note — the single mic. Tap → Right Hand / Field Hand opens (blurred one-surface overlay) and dictates. No second in-surface mic (fixes the v47 double-mic).
  • Media — Photos · Video · Other (misc), optional, after the note.

Canon

  • Writes to the per-(tenant × project × day) Daily Log (kerf_daily_log_canon).
  • Honest filing: "Saves to … today" → "Filed" only after the durable write.
  • Supersedes the v47 /field-capture heavy card.

Friendly job note

  • The spoken note renders as a model-written summary line + thumbnail + via voice chip + honest Filed · time — the artifact pattern from the 2026-06-01 packet, not a capture card.
