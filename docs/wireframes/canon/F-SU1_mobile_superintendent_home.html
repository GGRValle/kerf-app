F-SU1 · Mobile Superintendent Home · jobsite execution oversight

The Super's primary job: resolve drift on jobsites before it hits PM-level coordination, deploy crews tactically, verify quality, keep execution on track. This Home is operationally tilted — today's deployments lead, drift signals to resolve, quality gates pending, field crew comms. Cyan accent distinguishes Superintendent from PM (blue) and Field Hand (green).

F-SU1 · Spec

Role-root identity

  • 6th role-root · locked in Team+Ops walk 2026-05-18 PM
  • Operates between PM and Field Hand · jobsite execution oversight
  • Cyan accent (--super-cyan #5BBED2) · distinct from PM-blue and Field-green
  • Marcus Riley is GGR's Superintendent · canonical name

Canonical concerns vs PM Home

  • PM Home (F-P1) leads with decision-state rails (Movement · Who's Where · Holding · Changed)
  • Super Home leads with execution-state rails (Today's deployments · Drift to resolve · Quality gates · Crew comms)
  • PM thinks portfolio; Super thinks day-of-jobsite
  • PM coordinates with Owner; Super coordinates with field crews + subs

4 rails

  • Today's deployments · which crews at which sites · clock-in status · field-green accent
  • Drift to resolve · what needs Super's intervention today · amber accent (escalates to red on critical)
  • Quality gates · sign-offs · verifications · final walks · cyan accent (matches role color)
  • Field crew comms · messages from crew/subs requiring response · blue accent

One Thing · operational tilt

Super's One Thing is action-by-deadline, not decision-pending. "Resolve crew assignment by 5pm" not "Approve CO-002." Super deploys; doesn't approve.

Bottom bar swap

  • Home (active) · Projects · Speak · Schedule · More
  • Sales NOT in default · Super doesn't manage deals
  • Clients NOT in default · accessed via More if needed
  • Schedule is the swap-slot — Super lives in time-axis

Role visibility (from canon)

  • Crew (deployment authority over field crew) · full
  • Management (own projection) · full
  • Projects · heavy (jobs they oversee)
  • Schedule · heavy (deployment surface)
  • Clients · partial (deal-relevant clients only)
  • Sales · none
  • Money · partial (per-project budget only · no margin)
  • Marketing · none

Routes

  • One Thing CTAs → Wegrzyn Project Detail OR Hector direct comms
  • Deployment rail row → Project Detail with Field tab pre-selected
  • Drift rail row → Project Detail with Schedule tab pre-selected

What this surface enforces Execution-state lens. Super's day is jobsite-tactical: who's deployed where, what's slipping, what needs verification, what messages need answers. Not strategic decisions (PM's job). Not individual tasks (Field Hand's view). The rails are operational, the One Thing is action-by-deadline, the accent (cyan) reads distinct from PM-blue and Field-green.
What this surface refuses to be Not a PM Home variant. Not a portfolio dashboard. Not a Field Hand task list. F-SU1 is the jobsite-oversight projection of the same business graph PM and Owner see, scoped to what a Super resolves between strategy and execution.
