What this surface enforcesAllowance exceptions surface only the signal — the lines that drifted enough to matter. Over and under both visible; the founder reads net position from RH, then drills the biggest deltas. Red chip-tier only · no full-row red borders. Cross-project patterns flagged so a tile-$/sf drift on one job becomes a pricing-discipline lesson for the next bid.
What this surface refuses to beNot the Money Overview (cash position lives there). Not a per-job line-item ledger (drill to Project → Allowances for that). Not auto-approve — every variance over threshold requires founder touch. F-MN5b is the cross-project allowance exception scanner · 16 lines, both directions, signed deltas, drill-on-tap.
