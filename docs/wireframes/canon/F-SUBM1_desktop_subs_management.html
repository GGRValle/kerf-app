GC-side sub management (D-060 §6)

  • Roster of the GC's subs with active WOs + COI/insurance status at a glance.
  • Per-sub detail: WOs out, schedule, and a direct message thread.
  • Send a work order → lands in the sub's portal (F-SH2).

  • The comms thread is the other side of the F-SH2 portal, carried by the Right Hand relay.
  • The GC sees all their subs; each sub sees only its own slice — never other subs, clients, or money.

Placement

  • Lives on the merged PM / Super home (D-060 §5). Admin gets the compliance projection (COI/W-9/payment) of the same Sub.
  • Back control (non-home, §1).
