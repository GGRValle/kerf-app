J · APPROVAL-GATE AUTHORING · VOICE-FIRST at every permission threshold · approver can revise prices + lines · voice primary · multimodal fallback · diffed + logged
Principle: approval ≠ passive button. Whenever a CO, cost, or scope change hits a permission gate (owner ceiling, MoO threshold, insurance-restoration line, rebate-verified item), the approver gets a first-class authoring surface: talk the edit, Kerf redlines it, approver confirms. Every author action is diffed, reason-logged, Blackboard-persisted. Multimodal fallback: typed edit, camera for a doc/receipt, or resurface voice mode mid-edit.
